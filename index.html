This is

Astral Tides

Astral Tides is game made in the context of the 2016 edition of the university contest Ubisoft Montreal Game Lab Competition. It is also for some members of the team, their final project as student of NAD.
The objectif of the contest is to create a game prototype within 10 weeks with specific constraints such as following the theme "Ocean" and having 2 systems and 3 mecanics that interact with said systems.
Astral Tides is a third player view game, with a contemplative atmosphere in which you play as Qilak, an celestial being that came down to earth to get back the stars forming the constellation that fell on earth. As he take a wolf shape, he control the tides of water to aid him in his quest.
